Gym Bro is your AI-powered personal fitness coach. Tell it your goals, your
equipment, and your schedule — get a personalized weekly workout routine and a

• AI Workout Routines — Personalized weekly plans built around your goals,

• AI Diet Plans — Nutrition plans tailored to your body, targets, and
