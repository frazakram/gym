# Repsy — Product Brief

## What is Repsy?

Repsy is an AI personal trainer that lives in your phone. Built for the Indian gym-goer who's tired of generic Instagram workout plans and ₹3,000-per-month influencer programs that don't know your body, your gym, or your goals.

You tell Repsy three things — your goal, your equipment, and your body — and it builds you a complete weekly workout routine in under ten seconds. Then it tracks every set and every rep, and every Sunday it ships you a personalized progress report with an AI-written coach note.

## Why this app exists

Every fitness app in 2026 falls into one of three categories, and all three fail the user:

1. **Glorified spreadsheets** like Strong and Hevy — great if you already know what you're doing, useless if you don't.
2. **Pseudo-personal subscriptions** like BetterMe and Fitbod — their "personalization" is choosing from ten preset programs.
3. **Influencer PDFs** sold to fifty thousand people at once — the same plan for everyone, marketed as personal.

The breakthrough that makes Repsy possible: GPT-4-class vision models became cheap enough in 2025 to actually replace a junior personal trainer. They can read your body in a photo, identify the equipment in your gym, and adjust week over week based on what you actually did. That was impossible for the last decade. It's possible now. Repsy is the app that ships it for India.

## The promise

- **In ten seconds**, you get a workout plan that's actually yours.
- **In seven days**, you get a coach who remembers everything you did.
- **In thirty days**, you stop wondering what to do at the gym.

## Business model

- **Free tier:** Full routine generation, workout tracking, AI diet plans.
- **Premium (₹299 per month):** Weekly analytics report, muscle activation heatmap, AI coach notes, body composition tracking, gym equipment detection.
- **Payments:** Razorpay, INR-native, built for the Indian market.

## The five core differentiators

1. **Visual intelligence.** Uploads your gym photo, sees your equipment, never recommends a cable fly when you only own dumbbells.
2. **Body-aware planning.** Reads your body composition from a photo, factors it into training volume, intensity, and progression.
3. **Memory across weeks.** Every new routine knows what you did last week. Most apps reset weekly. Repsy remembers.
4. **Honest analytics.** MET-based calorie estimates, not the fake "45 calories per exercise" math every other app uses.
5. **Made for India.** Razorpay billing, Indian gym equipment recognition, INR pricing, English plus Hindi-friendly tone.
