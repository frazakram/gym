# Repsy — Competitive Positioning

## The competitive landscape (India, 2026)

| App | What they do well | Where they lose the user |
|-----|-------------------|--------------------------|
| **Strong, Hevy** | Best-in-class workout logging | No plan generation — you bring the plan. Useless for beginners. |
| **Fitbod** | Algorithmic routine generation | No visual intelligence, no body analysis, US-focused, ₹800-per-month pricing. |
| **BetterMe, Noom** | Heavy lifestyle marketing | Generic plans dressed as personal. Marketing-first, fitness-second. |
| **Cult.fit** | Strong India brand, live classes | Class-based, not routine-based. Doesn't make a personal plan for you. |
| **Influencer PDFs (₹2k–₹5k)** | Feel personal | Same PDF sold to thousands. Static. Never adapts. |
| **Hiring a personal trainer** | Real personalization, real accountability | ₹15,000 to ₹40,000 per month. Most Indians can't sustain it for six months. |

## Where Repsy sits

Repsy is positioned as the AI personal trainer at one-fiftieth the cost of a real one.

- Cheaper than a single influencer PDF after one month
- Fifty times cheaper than a real personal trainer
- More personalized than every app in the market because of gym detection plus body analysis
- Made for India — Razorpay billing, Indian gym equipment recognition, INR pricing, English plus Hindi-friendly tone

## The three sentences competitors cannot say

1. "Show me your gym, and I'll only recommend exercises you can actually do." — visual intelligence is the moat.
2. "I remember what you did last week, and what worked." — cross-week memory is rare.
3. "Every Sunday, I'll show you exactly what muscles you trained and what you missed." — most apps lack this honest feedback loop.

## Why now?

Two things converged in 2025 to 2026:

1. **The technology became cheap enough.** GPT-4-class vision models dropped to fractions of a cent per call. Per-user visual analysis became economically viable for the first time.
2. **The Indian market grew.** Indian gym memberships grew twenty-eight percent year over year post-COVID. The white-collar segment that wants premium fitness tools is at an all-time high.

The market is bigger than ever. The technology to serve it personally is finally affordable. Repsy is the app that ships at the intersection.

## What we are not

- We are not a "lose ten kilos fast" app — no fake transformations
- We are not a yoga and wellness app — we are a strength and fitness app
- We are not a social network — we don't have feeds, likes, or comments
- We are not a calorie-counting app — we estimate honestly, we don't obsess

## What we are

A focused, intelligent training app. One job: tell you what to do at the gym this week. Done well.
