# Repsy — Walkthrough Script

This is the suggested narrative spine for the NotebookLM-generated video. The AI may rephrase, but use these as the anchoring beats and language.

## Target length

Final cut: 75 to 90 seconds after editing.
NotebookLM will likely generate something longer — trim during post.

---

## Scene-by-scene script

### Scene 1 — Hook (0:00 to 0:08)

Open on the problem the user feels every week.

> Every fitness plan you've ever downloaded was written for somebody else. Generic templates. Influencer PDFs. Subscription apps with ten preset programs, and one of them is supposed to be yours. That isn't personalization. That's a guess.

### Scene 2 — Introducing Repsy (0:08 to 0:15)

> Repsy is different. It's an AI personal trainer that builds your weekly workout in ten seconds. Based on your goal. Your gym. And your body.

### Scene 3 — Feature: gym detection (0:15 to 0:25)

> Show it a photo of your gym. It sees the squat rack, the dumbbells, the cable machine — and only recommends exercises you can actually do.

Visual cue: cut to real screen recording of the gym photo upload and equipment detection.

### Scene 4 — Feature: body analysis (0:25 to 0:35)

> Upload a body photo, privately. The AI reads your composition, identifies what needs work, and balances your plan accordingly. Most apps treat every user the same. Repsy actually sees you.

Visual cue: cut to real screen recording of body composition analysis.

### Scene 5 — Feature: routine generation (0:35 to 0:45)

> Tap one button. In ten seconds, a complete weekly plan appears. Real exercises. Real sets and reps. Built for your level — not someone else's.

Visual cue: routine generates, weekly grid fills in day by day.

### Scene 6 — Feature: weekly report (0:45 to 1:05)

> Every Sunday, Repsy delivers your weekly report. A muscle activation heatmap shows what you actually trained. A push-pull-legs split tells you what you missed. And a short note from your AI coach calls out the one thing to fix next week.

Visual cue: weekly report scroll — heatmap, donut chart, AI coach note typing in.

### Scene 7 — The promise (1:05 to 1:20)

> No generic plans. No fake transformations. Just an app that knows you, remembers what you did, and shows up every Sunday with a plan for what's next.

### Scene 8 — Call to action (1:20 to 1:30)

> Repsy. Your trainer, generated. Free for the first seven days.

Visual cue: logo, App Store and Play Store badges, URL.

---

## Notes for NotebookLM

- Use a single narrator voice. Do not use the two-host conversational podcast format — this is an ad, not a discussion.
- Pace conversationally, not rushed. Pauses between scenes are good.
- Do not include music or sound effects in the export. Those will be added in editing.
- Do not show walls of text on slides. Each slide should have one headline and one supporting line maximum — the screen recordings of the real app will replace generic slides during editing.
